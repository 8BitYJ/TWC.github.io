ABOUT

The Technology Workforce Collaborative is a gathering of the Bay Area Technology Groups and Educational institutions.

The unifying goal of the collective groups is to increase education and networking opportunities for individuals throughout their career lifecycle.

The Technology Workforce Collaborative summit is an event designed to bring awareness of the various groups available to individuals as well as highlight the many educational and networking opportunities available.
